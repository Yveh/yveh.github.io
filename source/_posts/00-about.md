## About

I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science at **Rutgers University**, advised by [Prof. Dong Deng](https://people.cs.rutgers.edu/~dd903/). My research focuses on large-scale data management, with recent work on near-duplicate text alignment, locality-sensitive hashing, and similarity search.

Before Rutgers, I received my B.S. in Computer Science from **Shanghai Jiao Tong University**, where I was a member of the ACM Honors Class. I have also worked on streaming algorithms at Nanyang Technological University and on lattice cryptography at SJTU.
